About

I am second-year Ph.D. student at The University of Texas at Austin. I am co-advised by Venkat Arun and Işıl Dillig. My current interest lies on formal verification and automated synthesis of networked systems.

Previously, I graduated with major of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, and Mathematics at Johns Hopkins University. I am fortunate to work with Ryan Huang at The University of Michigan - Ann Arbor on improving the reliability of large-scale distributed system.
